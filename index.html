My name is Yu-Cheng Deng 鄧宇成. I am a master's candidate majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the University of California, Davis. I am currently a graduate student in Marcu Lab from Biomedical Engineering Department at the University of California, Davis. My research project includes fluorescence lifetime imaging (FLIm) data visualization, designing Graphical User Interface (GUI) for FLIm map construction, and Real-Time visualizatioon for FLIm data. My research interests include data analysis and visualization, image processing, computer vision and its applications.
I received my bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering from National Taipei Univeristy of Technology, Taipei, Taiwan. Throughout the life as an undergraduate student, I actively joined lots of school activities and was a CEO in EE student association. I held 2016 NTUT EE camp, 2016 EE uniform party as well as Student Association Evaluation as a general coordinator. Also photography and online games are my favorite pastimes and I enthusiastically welcome everyone to share any gorgeous photo with me.
